TradeSignals Backtest

Walks the last ~55 trading days bar-by-bar with the exact live engine. Decisions at 10:30 & 2:00 ET, one trade/day max, exit at next day's close or 0.75×ATR stop.
How to read this: "Win rate" is the share of trades that made money. "vs Hold" compares the engine's total return to just buying the stock on day one and holding. "Chop trades" are trades taken on days where the stock's realized range ended below 0.65× its average — the engine should take few of these, and they should lose little. Trading costs/slippage are not modeled.
